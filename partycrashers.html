Lux

Character Info
Name Lux
HP 100
Role Survivor

"What is this place?"

Overview: Lux is a small child trapped inside PARTY CRASHERS after entering through a dream. They rely on hope and survival instincts rather than strength, shown by their survivalist kit.

Lore: Lux believes this world is not real and holds onto the idea that escape is possible someday...

  • Dash: A boost that makes Lux fly forward, if the killer is put in their path, deal 25 HP and stun for 3 seconds.
  • Cover: Stands still and cowers in fear. If attacked during this peirod, stun for 3 seconds and deal 25 HP.

Toko

Character Info
Name Toko
HP 150
Role Fighter

"I'm not afraid of you!"

Overview: Toko is yet another character that is stuck in PARTY CRASHERS. Unlike Lux, he is not afraid of the dangers that are here...

Lore: Instead of hoping for escape, Toko focuses on finding flaws of this dream and fighting their way out.

  • Slash: Toko will stand still and charge up a Slash. Once used, stun for 5 seconds and deal 50 HP if hit.
  • Parry: Similar to Lux’s cower move, Toko will stand still and block any attacks for 4 seconds.
  • (PASSIVE) Bleed: Due to the scars on Toko, he will constantly take tick damage. Make sure you have a Medic on your team, or you wont survive.

Medic

Character Info
Name Medic
HP 100
Role Support

Medic as they appear in-game.

Overview: Medic is a vessel in PARTY CRASHERS that keeps others alive using heals, relying on speed and awareness rather than combat.

Lore: Fear drives Medic forward. They believe teamwork is the only path to escape.

  • Heal: Heals other survivors near you. If hit during this peirod, deal 2X damage.
  • Self Heal: Heals yourself up to 45 HP. If hit during this peirod, deal 2X damage.
  • (PASSIVE) Terrified: Medic has lower cooldowns and has a faster walk speed, but takes 1.15X Damage.

EVIL Lux

Character Info
Name Evil Lux
HP 666 (EVIL)
Role Killer

"That almost stung!"

Overview: Evil lux is an EVIL version of Lux that hunts down players and kills them, causing the game to reset once more.

Lore: Not much is known about this guy. Some believe he is a creation of the dream, others believe he is just a joke character, yet nothing has been directly stated by developers.

  • M1: Hits any player for 25 HP.
  • Beatdown: Rushes foward and attacks anyone in his path for 40 HP.
  • Self Regen: Stands still and regenerates for 5 seconds. During this time, any stuns deal twice the damage.

Style Notes

  • Lux & Medic: Players often pair Lux with Medic because Lux’s defensive abilities create safe moments for healing. Many fans see them as representing hope and protection.
  • Lux & Toko: Lux avoids direct fights while Toko runs toward danger, making them feel like opposites that balance each other during matches.
  • Toko & Medic: Toko’s aggressive playstyle gives Medic opportunities to support from behind, although some players joke that Medic spends most runs chasing Toko around the map.
  • Evil Lux & Lux: A major fan theory claims Evil Lux is a corrupted future version of Lux created by too many resets, though others believe it is simply the dream copying survivors.
  • Evil Lux Behavior: Players report that Evil Lux feels more aware than other killers, especially due to teleporting directly toward targets.
  • Team Symbolism Theory: Many fans interpret the survivors as emotional archetypes: hope (Lux), defiance (Toko), and fear-driven care (Medic).
  • Reset Theory: Some players claim subtle animation or behavior changes suggest characters evolve across multiple resets.
  • Unknown Connection: A popular speculation suggests Lux, Toko, and Medic may have known each other before entering PARTY CRASHERS, but no confirmed lore exists.
  • Homer (Developer Character): Homer is confirmed to be a developer-only joke character and is NOT obtainable in normal gameplay. Any sightings in public matches are assumed to be developer testing or bugs.
  • Homer Rumors: Community rumors about secret unlock methods or hidden codes to play as Homer are false according to developer notes.

Community Speculation

  • Some fans believe Evil Lux exists to punish players who rely too heavily on Lux strategies.
  • There is debate over whether the dream world creates killers from survivors or if killers exist independently.
  • A recurring theory suggests hidden events may occur when Lux, Toko, Medic, and Evil Lux appear within the same match.
  • Developers occasionally referencing Homer in updates has led to jokes that he is "watching from outside the game."

These notes may contain outdated information due to resets, patches, or developer testing.
