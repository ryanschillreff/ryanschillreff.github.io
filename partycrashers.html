Toko

Character Info
Name Toko
HP 150
Role Fighter

"I'm not afraid of you!"

Overview: Toko is yet another character that is stuck in PARTY CRASHERS. Unlike Lux, he is not afraid of the dangers that are here...

Lore: Instead of hoping for escape, Toko focuses on finding flaws of this dream and fighting their way out.

  • Slash: Toko will stand still and charge up a Slash. Once used, stun for 5 seconds and deal 50 HP if hit.
  • Parry: Similar to Lux’s cower move, Toko will stand still and block any attacks for 4 seconds.
  • (PASSIVE) Bleed: Due to the scars on Toko, he will constantly take tick damage. Make sure you have a Medic on your team, or you wont survive.

Medic

Character Info
Name Medic
HP 100
Role Support

Medic as they appear in-game.

Overview: Medic is a vessel in PARTY CRASHERS that keeps others alive using heals, relying on speed and awareness rather than combat.

Lore: Fear drives Medic forward. They believe teamwork is the only path to escape.

  • Heal: Heals other survivors near you. If hit during this peirod, deal 2X damage.
  • Self Heal: Heals yourself up to 45 HP. If hit during this peirod, deal 2X damage.
  • (PASSIVE) Terrified: Medic has lower cooldowns and has a faster walk speed, but takes 1.15X Damage.

EVIL Lux

Character Info
Name Evil Lux
HP 666 (EVIL)
Role Killer

"That almost stung!"

Overview: Evil lux is an EVIL version of Lux that hunts down players and kills them, causing the game to reset once more.

Lore: Not much is known about this guy. Some believe he is a creation of the dream, others believe he is just a joke character, yet nothing has been directly stated by developers.

  • M1: Hits any player for 25 HP.
  • Beatdown: Rushes foward and attacks anyone in his path for 40 HP.
  • Self Regen: Stands still and regenerates for 5 seconds. During this time, any stuns deal twice the damage.
  • Peekaboo: Teleports behind a random survivor.

Style Notes

This section contains player observations, fan theories, and archived community discussion. Some information may be speculative or outdated due to resets. [citation needed]

  • Lux & Medic: Early strategy threads frequently recommend pairing Lux with Medic due to strong defensive synergy. Fans often describe the duo as representing “hope protecting fear,” though developer intent is unknown. [citation needed]
  • Lux & Toko: Considered gameplay opposites by many players. Lux avoids confrontation while Toko actively seeks it, leading to a balanced dynamic when used together. Some discussions suggest this contrast may reflect deeper narrative themes.
  • Toko & Medic: A high-risk pairing where Medic supports aggressive engagements initiated by Toko. Veteran players joke that Medic mains become experts at pathing simply from following Toko across maps.
  • Evil Lux & Lux: One of the oldest fan theories claims Evil Lux is a corrupted future version of Lux created through repeated resets. Alternative theories suggest the dream world copies survivors into hostile forms. No official confirmation exists.
  • Evil Lux Behavior: Players frequently report that Evil Lux appears more aware than other killers, especially when teleporting toward targets. Some believe this is intentional lore design rather than AI behavior. [citation needed]
  • Team Symbolism Theory: A common interpretation assigns emotional archetypes to survivors: hope (Lux), defiance (Toko), and fear-driven care (Medic).
  • Reset Theory: Certain fans claim subtle animation or behavioral changes imply characters retain memories across resets. Evidence remains anecdotal but widely discussed.
  • Unknown Connection: Popular speculation suggests Lux, Toko, and Medic may have known each other before entering PARTY CRASHERS, though no confirmed lore supports this.
  • Blux (Developer Character): Confirmed developer-only test character. Sightings in public matches are generally assumed to be developer testing or bugs.
  • Blux Rumors: Claims of secret unlock methods or hidden codes continue to circulate despite repeated developer denials.
